# Compshop-Store-Data-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)

## Project Objective
The CompShop wants to create an annual sales report for 2025. So that, the owner of the store can understand their customers and grow more sales in 2026.

## Dataset
- <a href="https://github.com/LeonatShehu/Data-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Dataset-Shitje-Kompjuterike.xlsx">Dataset

## Questions (KPIs)
- Measures total revenue
- Measures overall profitability
- Monthly Sales	Tracks performance trends
- Monthly Profit	Measures profit seasonality
- Product Category Sales %	Identifies best-selling categories
- Number of Products Sold	Measures sales volume
- Sales by Region/City Shows geographic performance

- Dashboard Interaction https://github.com/LeonatShehu/Data-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Dataset-Shitje-Kompjuterike.xlsx

## Process
- Verify data for any missing values and anomalies, and sort out the same.
- Made sure data is consistent and clean with respect to data type, data format and values
- Created pivot tables according to the questions asked.
- Merge all pivot tables into one dashboard and apply slicer to make dynamic.

## Project Insight
December is the peak sales month. Laptops and accessories drive the majority of sales. Prishtina and Ferizaj are the strongest regions. Profit closely follows sales performance, indicating stable margins. Growth opportunities exist in low-performing cities and product categories

## Final Conclusion
Sales show clear seasonal patterns, with significant peaks in summer and year-end months.
Promotional campaigns or product launches during these high-performing months are likely effective. The business relies heavily on Laptops and Accessories, which together represent nearly half of all product sales. Gaming and Monitor categories also perform well, making them important revenue drivers. Major sales activity is concentrated in urban and highly populated regions. Lower-performing regions may require targeted marketing or improved distribution strategies. The company is financially healthy, with strong profitability and consistent growth patterns. Seasonal fluctuations are normal but manageable with proper inventory and marketing planning.
